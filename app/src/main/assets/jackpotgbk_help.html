About Jackpot GBK

Jackpot GBK (Gunting, Batu, Kertas) is an unique twist to the original "Rock, Paper, Scissors" game (also known as "Roshambo"). Regular "Rock, Paper, Scissors" rules apply, but with additional constraints.

Here, you cannot simply choose 🗿 Rock, 📃 Paper, or ✂️ Scissors. In order to choose, you must match at least two out of 3 icons (using the Start/Stop button).

  • 🗿🗿🗿: Allowed (Awarded 🗿 twice)
  • 📃✂️📃: Allowed (Awarded 📃)
  • 🗿📃✂️: Forfeit (Awarded ❌)

If you are able to match three, the matched item will be matched to your opponent twice. Unfortunately, if you are unable to match, you will be given a ❌ Forfeit which will increase the opponent's score, unless if the opponent does the same.

This game is based on the original Jackpot GBK game (https://github.com/reinhart1010/jackpotgbk) which was written in JavaScript. The Android version of this game does not include multiplayer (more than 2 players) support, and mostly rewritten in Java from scratch.
